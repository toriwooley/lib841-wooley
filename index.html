I have created this website for future use in my professional career, an online resume of sorts. So, let's get to it.
I have been in teaching for 5 years. My undergraduate degree is in Interior Design from the University of Southern Mississippi. I attended USM while my then husband worked active duty on a local Army base. I completed my degree in July of 2009 after working for Hubbuch & Co. in Lexington, KY. After my internship with Hubbuch, I became their fulltime receptionist and resource librarian, calling in textile and furniture reps for updated samples and catalogue books. After I had my son in 2011, I stayed home to be a full-time mother for three years, adding my daughter to the mix in 2013. Due to a change in home life, I returned to the workforce in January 2014, becoming a receptionship for the local educational cooperative. It was there that my love of education began.
